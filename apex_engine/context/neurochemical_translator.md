# System Tool: Neurochemical Effects Translator
**Filename:** `context/neurochemical_translator.md`
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Role:** Translate neurological/psychological concepts to musical descriptors

## 1. Purpose
This tool translates abstract neuropsychological and neurochemical concepts into concrete musical/acoustic descriptors that the MiniMax Music v2 model can interpret.

## 2. Neuropsychological Effects Dictionary

### Frisson (Musical Chills)
**Input Keywords:** frisson, chills, goosebumps, peak experience
**Translation:**
- Sudden dynamic crescendo
- Soaring harmony with unexpected resolution
- Layered vocal harmonies entering dramatically
- Key modulation at climactic moments
- Wide stereo imaging suddenly expanding

### Tension
**Input Keywords:** tension, anticipation, suspense, build
**Translation:**
- Rising melodic phrases
- Accelerating rhythmic patterns
- Filter sweeps opening upward
- Layered percussion building density
- Chromatic progressions

### Release
**Input Keywords:** release, resolution, drop, catharsis
**Translation:**
- Harmonic resolution to tonic
- Dynamic drop with space
- Open reverb tails
- Simplified texture after density
- Bass drop with sub frequencies

### Euphoria
**Input Keywords:** euphoria, ecstasy, joy, peak
**Translation:**
- Major key progressions
- Uplifting melodic contours
- Bright, airy production
- Triumphant brass or synth stabs
- Anthemic chorus structures

## 3. Neurochemical Targets Dictionary

### Dopamine (Reward/Anticipation)
**Input Keywords:** dopamine, reward, hook, addictive
**Translation:**
- Catchy, repetitive melodic hooks
- Syncopated rhythms creating prediction errors
- Call-and-response patterns
- Satisfying rhythmic resolutions
- Ear-worm vocal phrases

### Serotonin (Contentment/Calm)
**Input Keywords:** serotonin, calm, peaceful, content
**Translation:**
- Warm, rounded tones
- Major progressions with smooth voice leading
- Steady, predictable rhythms
- Soft, organic instrumentation
- Gentle dynamic curves

### Adrenaline (Excitement/Energy)
**Input Keywords:** adrenaline, energy, hype, intense
**Translation:**
- Fast tempos (140+ BPM)
- Aggressive drum patterns
- Distorted bass and synths
- Staccato, punchy elements
- High energy drops

### Endorphin (Pleasure/Bliss)
**Input Keywords:** endorphin, bliss, runner's high, pleasure
**Translation:**
- Euphoric build-ups
- Triumphant drops
- Soaring vocal melodies
- Lush pad textures
- Gradual intensity curves

## 4. Usage Example
**Input:** "Target dopamine and frisson at the chorus"
**Output:** "Catchy melodic hooks, syncopated bass, sudden crescendo, soaring harmony"
